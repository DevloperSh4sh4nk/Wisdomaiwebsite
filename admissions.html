Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions

What is the admission timeline?

The complete admission process takes 7-10 working days from application submission to confirmation.

Are there entrance exams?

We conduct a simple online assessment to understand the student's academic level and learning needs.

Can I change curriculum after admission?

Curriculum changes are allowed within the first 30 days, subject to availability and assessment.

What if my child misses a class?

All classes are recorded and available for 30 days. Students can catch up at their convenience.
